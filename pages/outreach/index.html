/ xander huggins's research wesbite

here are some scientific outreach initiatives I have participated in and led

  • Panel member in UVic Water Research and the SDGs: A Panel Discussion (2022). Watch the panel discussion here.

  • Participating scientist in the inaugural Water Day on the Hill (2020). An event that brought together water scientists across Canada to meet with parliamentarians and senior federal officials to raise awareness around water security in Canada. Read the report about the day of activities here.

  • Circle of Blue (2020). Perspective: How does the coronavirus pandemic compare to the global groundwater crisis? Read the thought-piece here.

  • Water availability stripes. Access the data visualisation here. A science communication tool and fun data-vis project to bring awareness to recent trends in water availability around the world.

  • Managing editor (2016-2021) of the Water Underground blog. Water Underground is a global collective of hydrogeologic researchers for water resource professionals, academics and anyone interested in groundwater, research, teaching and supervision. The blog is hosted on both the EGU Blogs and the AGU blogospheres. Read the blog here.
